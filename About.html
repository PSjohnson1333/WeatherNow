About WeatherNow

Welcome to WeatherNow, your reliable source for free, on-demand weather information!

Our mission is to provide accurate and easy-to-understand weather forecasts and data to help you plan your day, stay safe, and explore the fascinating world of meteorology.

Data Sources

We primarily utilize data from the following sources:

  • National Weather Service (NWS) API: For forecasts, alerts, and observational data within the United States.
  • Positionstack API: For geocoding location searches.
  • RainViewer API: For radar map tile overlays.
  • Google Gemini API: For AI-powered insights and explanations.
  • OpenStreetMap: For base map tiles.

Development

WeatherNow was developed with a passion for technology and accessible weather information. We strive to continually improve the site and add new features.
